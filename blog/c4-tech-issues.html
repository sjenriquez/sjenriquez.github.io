Diversity in tech

Much to be improved

11/1/14

It's no secret that the tech industry is largely white and male. Women and minorities are largely under-represented in this industry when compared to national statistics. Here are some charts to illustrate this data.

Contrasted with national labor statistics, numbers in tech are definitely behind:

2013 US Equal Employment Opportunity Commission National Aggregate Report

  • Men: 52%
  • Women: 48%
  • White: 64%
  • Asian: 6%
  • Black: 14%
  • Hispanic: 14%

Pouring over the numbers reveals the lack of representation among women and ethnic minorities. This is a very complicated issue and simple solutions do not suffice. Although, the tech industry requires specialized education. Exposing kids to this content early could help shift the workforce dynamics and representation.
