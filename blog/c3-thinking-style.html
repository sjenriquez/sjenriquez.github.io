So far so good

DBC Takes Time

10/24/14

My experience in Phase 0 at DBC has been great so far. My previous experience in HTML/CSS combined with studying basic Ruby for the interviews and application process has made Phase 0 pretty straight forward. The biggest challenge for me is time. Specifically, balancing a full-time job with the necessary DBC time. I'm thinking of it as practice for Phase 1 (and 2). I know that the workload will ramp up very quickly when the in-person phases begin. I've been using Phase 0 mostly as time management practice. So far so good!

As things heat up with DBC I'll definitely keep in mind to practice healthy inner-monologues. The curriculum will definitely be challenging, frequently. It will provide many opportunities to practice staying positive and maintain a growth mindset. DBC introduced us to this that perceives challenge and failure as tinder for growth. A growth mindset sees our brains as ripe for growth instead of innately limited. We'll definitely be challenged to push our brains and maintain a healthy perspective.
